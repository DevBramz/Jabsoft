Universal Unit 3

The Universal Unit 3 is a GPS/GSM/GPRS geographic locator designed for public or private fleet management applications where high performance and reliability are primary considerations. The UU3 is delivered with a 2G modem as the standard option. A GSM+UTMS 3G modem is available under a separate part number and pricing. Featuring internal antennas, the UU3 is a compact, easy-to-install vehicle tracking device. Designed for motor bikes, the UU3 provides real-time motor bike location and enhanced rider security features including automatically triggered hijack alerts.


The UU3 supports up to 2 RIM devices for enhanced anti-theft security, while WorldFleetLog can also support an additional tracking device hidden in the motor bikes. The Universal Unit 3 is completely integrated with EDT’s WorldFleetLog Web-based tracking and monitoring solution. Input from the Universal Unit 3 displays the real-time on-map motor bike location. WorldFleetLog can then generate real time alerts and reports regarding motor bike location, rider behavior, and a wide variety of business management oriented reports..


Universal Unit 3 Key Features

  • 24/7 Real-time Motor Bike Location and Security

  • Supports vehicle tracking options in Standby mode

  • Park, movement, theft, idle events, and battery alerts

  • Supports remote vehicle immobilizing by external relay

  • Remote horn and buzzer activation

  • SMS activation and deactivation of featured alerts

  • iButton and RFID identification for riders and passengers

  • All FMS events including rider behavior
