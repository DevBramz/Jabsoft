Time and Attendance Systems

Automating time and attendance for your organization with our time and attendance module which can seamlessly integrate with a wide range of payroll and range of payroll and HR management systems. JABSOFT AFRICA offers an open ended and customizable time and attendance system that will allow you easy and efficient control of attendance and punctuality of all employees in your company. With the use of the popular biometrics or badge readers, employees' attendance will be monitored and a wide range of management reports generated based on these transactions. Managers can remotely monitor and get up to the second reports regarding employee time and attendance from anywhere anytime.

Key Benefits

Accurate time recording information for specific areas
Ability to use the data stored for management purposes
Potential to link into automated payments for payroll
No preparation and distribution of time cards
Time-card information is calculated automatically by user defined criteria
No illegible punch registrations
No manual recording of exceptions and exception History
Historical reports provide access to valuable historical information
No problem processing labour reports on time. The system calculates as employs punch, eliminating' 'pay period crunch history
